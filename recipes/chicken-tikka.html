Chicken Tikka

Description

Chicken Tikka is a popular indian dish that is marinated in a delicious blend of spices and yogurt. The chicken is then grilled and served with a side of naan or rice. It is a great dish to make for a party or a family dinner.

Ingredients

  • boneless chicken thighs
  • greek yogurt
  • ginger garlic paste
  • garam masala
  • lemon juice
  • red chili powder
  • coriander powder
  • kasuri methi
  • mustard oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and marinate for 2 hours.
  2. Skewer the chicken and grill for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Serve with naan or rice.

Enjoy!
