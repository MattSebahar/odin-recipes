Naan

Description

Naan is a popular Indian flatbread that is perfect to serve with any curry. It is soft, fluffy, and has a slight chew. It is a great alternative to rice and can be used to scoop up any leftover curry sauce.

Ingredients

  • all-purpose flour
  • active dry yeast
  • sugar
  • salt
  • plain yogurt
  • water
  • ghee
  • garlic
  • coriander

Steps

  1. Combine yeast, sugar, and water in a bowl and let it sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Combine flour, salt, and yogurt in another bowl.
  3. Combine the two mixtures and knead for 10 minutes.
  4. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  5. Roll out the dough and cook on a skillet for 2 minutes on each side.

Enjoy!
